03
The studio
Note from the founders

Most studios will tell you they're obsessed with detail. We are, but the more useful claim is that we are obsessed with patience — the slow kind, the kind that only pays off in year three.

We were founded in 2019 by two book designers who'd grown tired of arguing for kerning over zoom calls. The studio has stayed deliberately small — seven people, one printer, one part-time dog — and we take on roughly nine projects a year.
We work in long arcs. A typical engagement runs ten to fourteen weeks, and most of our clients return for a second or third project. Identity work sometimes runs into typeface design; editorial work sometimes runs into architecture. We follow the thread.
